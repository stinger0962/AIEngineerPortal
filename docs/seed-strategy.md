# Seed Content Strategy

Phase 1 relies on deterministic seed content stored in Python seed builders.

## Content targets

- 7 learning paths with 5 lessons each
- 6 curated courses
- 48 exercises across Python/AI engineering categories
- 32 knowledge articles
- 6 project templates
- 24 interview questions

## Principles

- Keep content concise but practical.
- Personalize examples toward an experienced React/full-stack engineer.
- Prefer portfolio-building, shipping, and interview relevance over research-heavy theory.
