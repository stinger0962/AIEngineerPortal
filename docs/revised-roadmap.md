# Revised Roadmap

## North Star

Build a real AI engineer operating system that helps one user:

- learn the right material deeply
- practice it repeatedly
- build proof through projects
- track real market signals
- prepare for interviews
- close skill gaps continuously

This roadmap treats content depth as a first-class product track, not a side effect of feature work.

## Phase 0: Foundation

- Monorepo, app architecture, database, deployment, route map, docs

## Phase 1: Portal Core

- Dashboard, learning, courses, practice, knowledge, projects, interview modules
- Progress tracking and baseline seeded content structure

## Phase 2: Market Intelligence

- News and jobs ingestion
- Signal scoring, fit analysis, saved signals, dashboard intelligence

## Phase 3: Career Transition Engine

- Personalized interview roadmap
- Portfolio-readiness scoring
- Skill-gap surfacing and activity-aware next actions

## Phase 4: Real Curriculum Build

- Rewrite lessons into real teachable material
- Expand courses into coherent multi-week tracks
- Deepen practice with hints, solutions, progression, and review loops
- Upgrade knowledge content into durable references
- Strengthen interview packs and project blueprints so they align with the curriculum
- Make time estimates honest enough to trust for real study planning

## Phase 5: Adaptive Learning System

- Personalize curriculum based on progress, gaps, saved signals, and activity history
- Track mastery, not only completion
- Generate targeted next steps from weak areas

## Phase 6: AI Study Copilot

- Semantic search across lessons, knowledge, projects, jobs, and signals
- Conversational assistant grounded in portal content and user state
- On-demand explanation, comparison, brainstorming, debugging, and review help

## Phase 7: Evidence and Job Conversion

- Resume-to-job alignment
- Portfolio evidence scoring
- Project-to-resume bullet generation
- Job-specific prep packs and targeting workflows

## Phase 8: Production Hardening

- Admin/content tooling
- Release and deployment reliability
- Observability, backups, and longer-term security hardening

## Current Status

- Phase 0: complete
- Phase 1: structurally complete, but content depth still needs major improvement
- Phase 2: complete
- Phase 3: complete
- Phase 4: active

## Why Phase 4 Is Next

The product now has enough structure and intelligence to be useful, but it still needs substantially better authored learning material.

Phase 4 is where the portal shifts from:

- a smart-looking portal with outline-grade lessons

to:

- a real study environment with teachable lessons, realistic pacing, and practice that compounds over time
